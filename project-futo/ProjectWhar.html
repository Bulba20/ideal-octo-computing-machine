About Us

This tofu factory is one of the oldest tofu factories in Indonesia, that has lasted for more than 100 years. It has lasted three generations, and is a family business that started back in 1924. Over the years, it grew to be a popular store known for its organic and healthy way of making tofu; but most importantly, it was known for its delicious taste.
